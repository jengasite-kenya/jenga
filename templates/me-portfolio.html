About Kitonga

Founded in Nairobi in 2018, Kitonga Studio is a one-person practice devoted to the quiet drama of built space. No people, no clutter — just structure, material and the way Kenyan light moves across a room.

Work spans architecture firms, interior designers, hospitality and property developers across East Africa.
