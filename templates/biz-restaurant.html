Our story

From the coast
to your table

Ember began with a simple idea: that Kenyan food deserves a modern stage. We work with farms in the highlands and fishers on the coast to bring the freshest of the country onto one plate.

Every dish is fired over charcoal, plated with care, and built around the produce of the season. No shortcuts — just honest cooking.
