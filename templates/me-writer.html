About the writer

Imani Otieno is an essayist and former journalist based in Nairobi. Her work has appeared in regional anthologies and on stages from Kampala to Cape Town, always circling the same questions: what we keep, what we lose, and what it means to notice.

When she isn't writing, she is walking the city with a notebook she rarely opens.
