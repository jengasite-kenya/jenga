📡 Connecting your own domain

After you save your domain above, you must point it to SiteSawa at the company where you bought it (e.g. Truehost, Safaricom, GoDaddy). Log in there, open DNS settings, and add these two records:

Type: CNAME   Name: www   Value: sitesawa.com
Type: A      Name: @     Value: (ask SiteSawa for the IP)

⏱️ Changes can take a few minutes up to 24 hours to work. Your yourname.sitesawa.com address works instantly in the meantime. Stuck? Message us on support and we'll help you set it up.
